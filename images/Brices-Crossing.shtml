Brices Crossing-A great neighborhood in the Creekside Elementary school District

Realtor Steve Tyson

Brices Crossingis one of the premier neighborhoods in the New Bern-Havelock area . The reasons for this are many, and a few are mentioned below.

  • Brices Crossing is less than a 20 minute drive to the back gate of Cherry Point Marine Air Station.

  • Brices Crossing is less than 1/2 mile from the much desired Creekside Elementary School.

  • Brices Crossing is less than 10 minutes from historic downtown New Bern.

  • Brices Crossing has a wide variety of new and newer homes for sale.

  • Brices Crossing has a variety of home price points, starting in the low $150,000's.

  • Brices Crossing is within 15 minutes from the Croatan National Forest, over 150,000 acres of forest land open to the public.

  • Brices Crossing is only 5 minutes away from the Craven County's largest Recreation Park. Creekside Park has soccer fields, football fields, baseball fields, walking trails, fishing docks, kayak rentals, and gazebos with outdoor grills.

  • Brices Crossing is only 30-35 minutes from the beautiful beaches of the Crystal Coast.

  • Brices Crossing Is within a few minutes of Craven Regional Airport making traveling easy.

  • Brices Crossing is within a few minutes of 3 golf courses.

These are just a few of the reasons why Brices Crossing is such a great place to call home.
